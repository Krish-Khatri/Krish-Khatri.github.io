About me

Hello! I am Krish, a software engineer.

I have a passion for creating websites, applications and games utilizing different and innovative technologies. My goal is to always build products that provide stable and meaningful experiences.

I was born in the city of Udaipur located in Rajasthan, India. Growing up, computers and such always interested me. My parents bought me a personal laptop when I was in high school and I knew then that I wanted to pursue Computer Science as my career. I am currently studying Computer Science at The University of Alabama and will graduate May of 2021.

In college, I've been able to build my portfolio by working on a wide variety of interesting and meaningful projects on an almost daily basis. This has built up my proficiency in technologies currently used in the industry and allowed me to further hone my problem-solving skills.
